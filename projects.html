Task Nagger

Task naggest automates the task of nagging others to get a job done.

First, prepare a .csv file of people to be nagged, along with their emails, the chore they should be nagged about, and the frequency with which they should be nagged.

Schedule the program to run with a task scheduling tool such as Cron in Ubuntu and nagging emails will automatically be sent on your behalf!

 

Stock News Alerter

Stock News Alerter monitors the price of a particular stock and determines the difference current price and the closing price from the previous day.

If the difference in price exceeds a threshold value, Stock News Alerter compiles a summary of the top 3 news articles about the company from API News and sends and email to alert the user of change in price that includes relevant articles.
